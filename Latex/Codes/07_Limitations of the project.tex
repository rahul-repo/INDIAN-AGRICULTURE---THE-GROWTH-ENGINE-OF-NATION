\section{Limitations of the project}

i) The years we considered here are not same for every crop due to the unavailability of the data.

\vspace{2 mm}
\hspace{-5 mm}ii)In finding the correlation we have used the average temperature and rainfall data. But India is a large country , too much variation are seen between different regions of India. If in those cases we can use the local weather data that will give us more efficient result.

\vspace{2 mm}
\hspace{-5 mm}iii) During the calculation of future trend we are assuming that weather will be as before but with a large change in the amount of rainfall and temperature (though it has low chance of happening) it might affect the production.
