# Indian Agriculture and Growth Trends 

## Overview

This project analyzes historical agricultural production data for major crops in India to study long-term growth patterns, regional variation, and environmental influences on crop production. The study combines exploratory analysis, correlation methods, time-series modelling, and forecasting techniques to understand agricultural trends and future production prospects.

---

## Project Year: 2025

## Objectives

- Identify major crop production patterns in India
- Compare production levels across crops and regions
- Examine relationships between agricultural output and environmental factors
- Analyze long-term growth trends using historical data
- Develop forecasting models for future crop production

---

## Data

The analysis uses historical agricultural datasets containing:

- Crop-wise production statistics
- State and regional production records
- Rainfall measurements
- Temperature indicators
- Time-series agricultural observations

---

## Methodology

### Exploratory Data Analysis

- Summary statistics of crop production
- Regional comparison of agricultural output
- Identification of top-performing crops
- Visualization of production trends

### Correlation Analysis

- Relationship between crop production and rainfall
- Relationship between crop production and temperature
- Assessment of environmental influences on agricultural performance

### Time-Series Analysis

- Trend estimation using historical production data
- Study of long-term growth patterns
- Identification of production dynamics over time

### Forecasting

- Development of forecasting models for crop production
- Future yield projections based on observed trends
- Evaluation of production under environmental conditions
